A — 4-Tab Hub (Simplified)

Merge Alerts into Home as a feed. Home = dashboard + activity feed. Community = notices, chat, events, polls, directory. Services = visitors, bills, packages, facilities. More = profile, settings, admin. Fewer tabs = less decision paralysis.

B — Action-First Home (Recommended)

Home screen prioritizes things that need your attention — pending visitors, due bills, unread notices — with inline actions (approve/reject/pay). Quick access as a horizontal scrollable row. Community feed below. Most important stuff first, everything reachable in 1-2 taps.
